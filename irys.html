Fahim Abir

Against the cruel, unstoppable march of time, our greatest assets are the memories we make. We look for love and happiness, for fulfillment and warm smiles.
Life is a short, yet unfathomably epic journey. We never know where the flow would take us. And in this tide of time, we encounter very few individuals whose company makes us find the fulfillment we all look for, make memories we would cherish till the end of our days. And I count myself one of the luckiest travelers in this timeline to have encountered you and find your endearing love and care.
I made this website in an attempt to preserve some memories of the precious people in your life. In the fleeting pursuit of happiness, it’s the little moments that keep us going through the hard part, the seemingly endless struggle, hoping that we will find what we have been looking for at the end of the road.

What are we looking for, really?
Looking back at life, we realize that no matter how terribly tired we are, we have made it through a great portion of the journey. And we have all these great memories, pictures of great times, warm wishes from our friends and family, who love us no matter how little we have achieved or how far we have gone in this journey.

Happy Birthday, my dear Auritree ❤️
Thank you for being the love of my life, my partner in this journey, your smile keeps me going through the worst of days and radiates through the darkest of tunnels.
And I’ll always be here for you, hold your hand in mine, sing the songs of wishful dreams and walk the weary roads with spring in my steps.

I love you to infinity and beyond ❤️
